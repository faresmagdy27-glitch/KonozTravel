Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how Konoz Travel collects, uses, stores, and safeguards your data when you use our website or book our services.

Information We Collect

Konoz Travel may collect personal information including your name, email address, phone number, nationality, travel preferences, booking details, and any information necessary to provide our travel services.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process bookings, arrange travel services, communicate important updates, improve our services, and comply with legal obligations.

Sharing Your Information

We only share your information with trusted suppliers such as hotels, airlines, transportation providers, and destination partners when necessary to complete your reservation. We never sell your personal information.

Payment Security

Payments are processed securely through trusted payment providers. Konoz Travel does not store complete credit card information on its own servers.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and remember your preferences.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information where permitted by applicable laws.
